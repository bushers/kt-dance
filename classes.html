Classes

Not sure what class to go for? Check out our range of dance styles below for a description of each class to help you choose.

Classes include Ballet, Tap, Street, Commercial, Jazz & Disco, Baby Ballet, Musical Theatre, Drama, Ballroom and Latin.

Ballet

Ages 4 - 19

Pupils follow the IDTA ballet grades syllabus. Ballet is a great for building, strength, stamina, good posture and fluidity of movement. Ballet is a disciplined and technical dance style, pupils build confidence by taking OFQUAL regulated tests and also are given the opportunity to showcase their talents in shows and events.

Pupils can take Rosettes, Medals or Grade examinations.

Prices and Timetable

Baby Ballerina

Ages 2 1/2 - 4

Children will learn their first rudiments of ballet in a fun way, using different themes of the week, props to inspire learning. The little Dancers have a great time and are kept interested by using themes such as Princess week, Nature week, Teddy Bear, Sporty week, Birthday, Christmas, Easter etc.

Prices and Timetable

Ballroom and Latin

All ages

Learn the Waltz, Quickstep, Foxtrot and Tango; classic styles that are showcased on popular TV programmes like 'Strictly Come Dancing'. Dancing with a partner is a great skill to develop for socialising with new partners or choreographing a wedding dance.

Or learn the Cha Cha, Rumba, Jive and Samba. Latin dancing is an energetic partner dance that is great for socialising as well as keeping in shape.

Opportunities to take part in events and take ISTD examinations.

Prices and Timetable

Commercial and Street Jazz

All Ages

Pupils learn moves to the trendiest popular music. Learn cool funky moves as seen on screen. Style diversity within the classes as lots of different moods, speeds and dynamics according to the chosen music. Pupils can progress to take exams and take part in our shows/events.

Prices and Timetable

Musical Theatre

Ages 7 and above

This class will enable children to have fun learning the songs, dances and lyrics of their favourite musicals. These classes are also great for developing a childs confidence and imagination.

The acting in Musical Theatre links our theatre shows so the Actors have an opportunity to play lead roles. Pupils can also work towards Musical Theatre exams.

Prices and Timetable

Tap

Ages 4 - 16

Tap is an extremely rhythmic dance style. Tap is a fun dance style to learn, your feet sing to the beat of the music. Tap begins with a series of steps linked together and develops by linking more steps and sounds.
