Classes

Not sure what class to go for? Check out our range of dance styles below for a description of each class to help you choose.

Classes include Ballet, Tap, Street, Commercial, Jazz & Disco, Baby Ballet, Musical Theatre, Drama, Ballroom and Latin.

Ballet

Ages 4 - 16

These classes build strength, stamina, good posture and fluidity of movement. Ballet is a beautiful dance style, creating long lean muscles from the disciplined techniques.

Prices and Timetable

Baby Ballerina

Ages 2 1/2 - 4

Classes are themed around our class mascot Dancing Bear, flashcards, Dancing Bear songs and exercises. Children will learn their first rudiments of ballet.

Prices and Timetable

Ballroom and Latin

All ages

Learn the Waltz, Quickstep, Foxtrot and Tango; classic styles that are showcased on popular TV programmes like 'Strictly Come Dancing'. Dancing with a partner is a great skill to develop for socialising with new partners or choreographing a wedding dance.

Or learn the Cha Cha, Rumba, Jive and Samba. Latin dancing is an energetic partner dance that is great for socialising as well as keeping in shape.

Prices and Timetable

Commercial Disco and Street

All Ages

Street dance is covered within "The Dance School" Disco Street dance classes. Examination can be taken with ISTD and ITDA. We also offer a boys only class.

Prices and Timetable

Musical Theatre

Ages 7 and above

This class will enable children to have fun learning the songs, dances and lyrics of their favourite musicals. These classes are also great for developing a childs confidence and imagination.

Prices and Timetable

Tap

Ages 4 - 16

Tap is an extremely rhythmic dance style. Tap is a fun dance style to learn, your feet sing to the beat of the music. Tap begins with a series of steps linked together and develops by linking more steps and sounds.
